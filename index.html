We Are PulsePal

A startup developing a smartphone based blood
pressure monitor.

£2 Billion

That's the annual cost of high blood pressure to the NHS.

1 in 4 people have high pressure and many more do not know that they do. Although remote healthcare is here to stay, remote diagnostics has yet to catch up. Our software means that you can just open your camera and, hey presto! Get your vitals! We're using fundamental physics, signal analysis and ML to provide accurate and reliable information and are in the midst of negotiations to run large scale clinical trials.

The UK health and wellness market is worth £13 billion! We are capturing that market by providing a subscription service, partnering with doctors and tele-health companies, building APIs and using our data to provide consultancy services.

Value Proposition

The current cost of a GP appointment is £37 for a nine minute consultation.

The cost of a nurse home visit is £27 per visit. Given our platform purely uses software, during that 9 minute period we could have taken 18 blood pressure measurements at the cost of 10p per measurement.

During a remote consultation a doctor can use software either on the GPs end or the patient's end (either in the form of an app or an API) to monitor the patient's vital signs. Working with GPs we hope to collect data in order to improve our models to ensure ever improving accuracy and robustness.

What Have We Achieved So Far?

Currently we can obtain a patient's PPG (thus their heart rate and heart rhythm) from a video of their index finger. By applying signal processing to smartphone cameras we have achieved accuracy equivalent to the market leaders (a CE Marked Device). By expanding our dataset, we will be able to obtain reliable measurements for blood pressure, respiratory rate, and blood oxygen levels.
