We Are PulsePal

A startup developing a smartphone based blood
pressure monitor.

£2 Billion

That's the annual cost of high blood pressure to the NHS.

1 in 4 people have high pressure and many more do not know that they do. Although remote healthcare is here to stay, remote diagnostics has yet to catch up. Our software means that you can just open your camera and, hey presto! Get your vitals! We're using fundamental physics, signal analysis and ML to provide accurate and reliable information and are in the midst of negotiations to run large scale clinical trials.

The UK health and wellness market is worth £13 billion! We are capturing that market by providing a subscription service, partnering with doctors and tele-health companies, building APIs and using our data to provide consultancy services.
